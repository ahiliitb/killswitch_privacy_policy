KillSwitch – Privacy Policy

Effective date: 18 December 2025

KillSwitch is a digital wellbeing app that helps reduce screen time by monitoring app usage and blocking distracting apps. This app is designed for users of all ages, including children under 13, to help manage device usage.

1. Information We Collect

KillSwitch does not require an account and does not collect personal information like names, emails, or phone numbers.

To provide functionality, the app processes:

  • Installed apps and package names – to let you select apps to monitor or block
  • App usage statistics – which apps you use and for how long (processed on your device only)
  • Screen text content – if you enable AI features, we read on-screen text to classify content as distracting or productive
  • App settings and preferences – stored locally on your device

2. Third-Party AI Service (OpenAI)

If you enable AI features, the app sends screen text snippets and app names to OpenAI's API to classify content. We do not send your name, device ID, or contact information. Data is sent over encrypted HTTPS connections.

3. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored on your device only. We do not maintain cloud servers. When using AI features, data is temporarily sent to OpenAI for classification. You can delete all data by clearing app data in system settings or uninstalling the app.

4. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

KillSwitch is designed for users of all ages, including children under 13, to help reduce screen time.

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children
  • All data processing happens on-device or is limited to necessary AI classification
  • We do not sell data or use it for advertising
  • Parents/guardians can control permissions and delete data at any time

If you are a parent/guardian and have concerns, please contact us using the email on the app's store page.

5. Your Controls

You can:

  • Grant or revoke permissions in device settings
  • Enable or disable AI features
  • Configure which apps are monitored or blocked
  • Clear app data or uninstall to delete all information

6. Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact us using the email address on the app's store listing.
